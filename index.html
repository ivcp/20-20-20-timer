The 20-20-20 rule: Every 20 minutes of screen time, look at something that is about 20 feet (6 meters) away for a full 20 seconds.

Regular screen breaks give your eyes some much-needed rest and help prevent eye strain.

Look away from the screen when you hear the alarm. Continue working when the you hear the sound again.
